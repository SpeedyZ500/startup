    Project Yggdrasil (Organnization)

    This site is a part of Project Yggdrasil, it acts as "an access point into a location within the void, between realities." Created by Spencer Zaugg as a project for his CS 260 class at BYU, when he wanted to make a group authoring site where people could make branching timelines, he accidentaly created a window into the world of Imagination.

    History:

    Founded by Spencer Zaugg while he was creating a website for his CS 260 class at BYU. Somhow it began to be able to view and access information from another world and facilitate communication between worlds.

    Goals:

    Inspire Writers
    Writers help to stabelize realities. When a writer doesn't write a story idea they come up with, it causes multiversal collapse. Killing trillions of lives in the process, which can affect stories protected by the branches of Yggdrasil. Causing additional multiversal collapse events, however the more stories Yggdrasil contains the more stable it is. Some stories never written are apart of Yggdrasil, and protected by its branches, but can still be affected by multiversal collapse, unless The World Tree is strong enough to resist it.
    Chronicle the stories Within
    There are stories untold contained within Yggdrasil, these stories unwrritten are apart of Yggdrasil, when the writer gives up on them, they collapse. Chronicling them however strengthens Yggdrasil, and prevents them from experiensing collapse.
    Protect the inhabitants of Yggdrasil
    The people within the worlds within Yggdrasil may seem imaginary to us, however who are we to define who is real and who is not.
